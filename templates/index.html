xi.pe - URL Shortener & Pastebin

URL Shortener

Shorten your URLs with our simple API:

POST /api/urlpost?key=abcd1234&url=https://example.com

Then access your shortened URL at: xi.pe/abcd1234

API Usage

  • Shorten URL: /api/urlpost?key=yourkey&url=yoururl
  • Access URL: /yourkey (4-8 alphanumeric characters)
  • Statistics: /stats
